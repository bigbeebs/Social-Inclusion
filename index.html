Our Goals and Commitments

Development

We are committed to our community, and to developing programs that meet the needs of our students, whom we attract from all over the world.

Strategy

For individuals committed to social inclusion and development to apply their experiences and knowledge to areas of policy and practice, using an equity- and justice-oriented inclusive lens.

Engage

Engaging a transdisciplinary approach that allows us to acknowledge and examine the intersectional nature of our commitment across different areas of study, populations, communities, and research and practice.

Have a Voice

We offer a range of options such as a focus on human rights, engagement with our research program, and tools needed to make an impact for change..

Why Social Inclusion and Development?

Empowering communities locally, nationally, and internationally to advance wellness, educational access, economic participation, and social opportunities for all people by developing leaders, building knowledge, and demonstrating real-world innovations that embrace inclusion..

  • To focus our activities on empowering individuals to define their own quality of life and realize their full potential.
  • To recognize that this work requires advocating for and defending the rights of all individuals.
  • To undertake work that creates social change and promotes human rights and justice for individuals who are excluded from their community because of their race, ethnicity, gender,or other factors.
